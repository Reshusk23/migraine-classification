Migraine

Migraine is a common disabling primary headache disorder. Many epidemiological studies have documented its high prevalence and socio-economic and personal impacts. In the Global Burden of Disease Study2010 (GBD2010), it was ranked as the third most prevalent disorder in the world.   • Sporadic hemiplegic migraine
  • Familial hemiplegic migraine
  • Other (Not Migraine)
